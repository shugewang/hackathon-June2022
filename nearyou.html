SPORT CLUBS

PRiDE OUT

PRiDE OUT is a friendly and inclusive LGBT + cycling group based in Greater Manchester open to all cyclists in the northern England and beyond. PRiDE OUT welcomes everyone regardless of sexuality, age, gender, identity, bike or ability. Their aim is to challange homophobia, biphobia and transphobia in sport and improve access to sport for LGBT+ people.PRiDE OUT works towards these goals for campaign for change, educate, promote good practice and actively grow LGBT+ participation and satisfaction in sport.

Manchester Indoor Softball League

Manchester Indoor Softball Legue is a friendly mixed gender softball legue for adults 14+. Learn a new sport, make friends and stay fit for you basbell/softball season. EVERYONE IS WELCOME. You can find us at Powerlegue Trafford. Every tuesday night from 7-9pm.

Open Athletics

Open athletics is an organistaion for the promotion and developmeent of LGBT atheltics. Their aim is to be a forum and network of individual LGBT athletcs, groups and clubes across the UK.

OutdoorLads

OutdoorLads is a group which aims to improve the menthal and physical wellbing of gay, bisexual and trans men through outdoor pursuits in the UK and worldwide. Activities ranges from hiking, climbing, biking, canoeing, scrambling, running and sailing, as well as purely social events. Events are run by volunteers all over the UK.

Tae Kwon-Do at LGBT Foundation

Tae Kwon-Do accepts everyone to join their classes every Monday at 8pm at 5 Richmond St, M13HF. It is open to anyone aged 18+, all adults welcome, whatever previous exercise or no exercise expereince.

Northern Wave Swimming

Northen Wave Swimming believe in providing a safe, fun, accessible and inclusive LGBT swimming club in Manchester. All you require for your first lesson is your swimwear, a towel, a pair of goggles and water to drink.
